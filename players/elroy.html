Elroy Silvermoon

Silver Metallic Dragonborn Bard of the College of Creation

Background

Elroy Silvermoon, a Silver Metallic Dragonborn, moved from Daggerford to Waterdeep's Field Ward. He found employment at The House of Song, a small library in the Trade Ward, where he befriended the Gnomish bookkeeper, Sabbit.

Tragedy

Elroy's life took a dark turn when his wife, Nala, became involved with the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild. Framed for tax evasion, Elroy was eventually cleared but lost his daughter Talma to the guild's retaliation.

The Song of Creation

Desperate to save his daughter, Elroy discovered an ancient secret in the library - the Song of Creation. This discovery led him to pursue the path of a bard, harnessing the power of creation through his poetry and philosophy.

Connections

Despite his troubles, Elroy finds solace in his community. His neighbors include Miss Spud, a friendly elderly goblin, and Biv, a reclusive old man. These connections provide small comforts as he navigates the dangerous waters of Waterdeep's underworld.
