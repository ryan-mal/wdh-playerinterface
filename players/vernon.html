Vernon Margaster

Human Druid of the Circle of the Shepherd

A Father's Wrath

Vernon Margaster's early life was marked by the brutal treatment at the hands of his father, Farris Margaster. Amidst the violence, Vernon clung to a ticking pocket-watch, a symbol of hope in his darkest moments. His mother, Ember Margaster, became his sole protector in a household consumed by cruelty and fear.

A Mother's Sacrifice

In a daring act of love, Ember orchestrated Vernon's escape from the Margaster estate. With a final, tearful embrace, she sent him off with his loyal companion, Fidi, a small yellow canary. Little did Vernon know, this would be the last time he'd feel his mother's loving touch, as he fled into the shadowy streets of Waterdeep.

Nature's Embrace

Guided by Fidi, Vernon found sanctuary with the Emerald Enclave. Under the tutelage of Archdruid Aina Lefaliir, he discovered his innate ability to communicate with beasts and harness the power of nature. Vernon learned to transform himself, growing claws and fur, channeling his inherited anger into a force for protecting the natural world.

A Promise in the Shadows

Now a druid of the Circle of the Shepherd, Vernon walks a path between two worlds. He vows to use his newfound powers to free his mother from his father's clutches and defend the Emerald Enclave. In the shadowy underbelly of Waterdeep, Vernon seeks to unravel the mysteries of his past while forging a future that defies the legacy of his family name.
